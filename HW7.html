Glaucoma Information

What is Glaucoma?

Glaucoma is an eye condition where the optic nerve is damaged due to increased intraocular pressure. It can lead to vision loss and blindness.

Recognizing Glaucoma

Glaucoma often has no early symptoms, making regular eye exams crucial. Measurement of intraocular pressure and optic nerve examination are key for identification.

Caring for Yourself

If you have glaucoma, regular eye check-ups, following your doctor's advice, and maintaining a healthy lifestyle are essential.
